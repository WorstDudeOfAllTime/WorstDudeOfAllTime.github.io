Hello, I'm Kris!

I was born and raised in Upper Darby, Pennsylvania. I went through the Upper Darby school system until 11th grade at which point I switched to an online program in which I ultimately taught myself the last 2 years of high school. I attended Delaware County Community College for 2 years before transferring to Drexel University. After 2 terms, I left Drexel for financial reasons. If I am being honest, I was not cut out for college at that point in my life and I would end up not returning.

I've spent the last decade and a half bouncing from job to job. I've been a mailroom clerk, a dedicated corporate houseman, a line cook, a real estate agent, a junk removalist(?), a billboard photographer, an art handler, and more. I would consider myself a child of the internet and have always been intrigued by web design and development. I'd looked into bootcamps for a couple years and being a natural skeptic I told myself to avoid them. After discussing with some of my dev friends, I was convinced to jump in and now you are joining me on that journey.

In my personal life, I am a long time weight lifter and musician. Both have become more of a hobby, but ultimately music is a passion of mine. I spend a lot of time cycling around Philadelphia with a camera.
